"Clothes make the man"

At Dress to Impress, we believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to present their best self to the world. Our mission is to provide free high-quality, professional clothing to individuals in underserved communities, empowering them to approach job opportunities with confidence and dignity.

Transforming Lives One Pants At a Time

We understand that the right outfit can make all the difference in a job interview, a presentation, or any professional setting. That's why we're committed to breaking down barriers and ensuring that financial constraints never prevent someone from looking and feeling their absolute best.

Our History

Created in 2015, Dress to Impress began as a small community initiative when our founder, Karen Smith, noticed that many talented and underprivilledged individuals in her neighborhood were missing out on job opportunities simply because they couldn't afford to look proffesional and clean. What started as lending clothes to neighbors and the people in her community has grown into a thriving nonprofit serving over 5,000 individuals annually the city.

Our Mission

To empower individuals in underserved communities by providing high-quality, professional clothing at no cost, enabling them to approach job opportunities with confidence and dignity. We believe that everyone deserves the chance to present their best self to the world, regardless of their financial circumstances.

Our Vision

A world where professional success is not limited by access to appropriate clothing. We envision communities where every individual has the confidence and resources to pursue their career aspirations without barriers, creating a more equitable and inclusive workforce.
